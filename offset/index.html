Offset 2025 Art Book Fair

Hosted by Parsons CD
& Draw Down Books

October 25th, 11–4PM

Parsons School of Design
2 West 13th Street, 6th Floor

Support Us

Information

Parsons Communication Design is thrilled to announce the Second Annual Offset Art Book Fair, hosted in partnership with Draw Down Books. You'll find us in 2 W 13th St. on 5th Avenue– on the sixth floor's main atrium space.

Offset facilitates the exhibition & vending of posters, prints, books, and other ephemera; with the effort of highlighting and supporting small press publishing & zine-making culture.

The event will be prefaced by a keynote CD Lecture on Friday, October 24. You can keep up with our program by following our Instagram.

Registration to the fair is free– but we are following a timed ticket schedule. Advance ticket registration is required due to limited capacity in the building. For more information, contact us at offsetartbookfair@gmail.com.

Support Us

Parsons School of Design's Communication Design Department is seeking donations to keep Offset Art Book Fair up and running for many years to come. Your support directly aids the students, faculty, and staff behind Offset– and it's commitment to platforming & uplifting young bookmakers in NYC. If you wish to contribute, you can do so by donating to our GiveCampus Page and selecting 'Parsons Art, Media, and Technology' as a beneficiary. We hope to see you this October!
